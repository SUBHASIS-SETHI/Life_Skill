## 2. Tiny Habits by BJ Fogg – Core Message

### Question 2  
How can you use B = MAP to make new habits easier? What are M, A, and P?

- B = Behavior  
- M = Motivation  
- A = Ability  
- P = Prompt  
- Make habits easier by lowering ability requirements (make it tiny) and using strong prompts instead of relying only on motivation.

### Question 3  
Why is it important to "Shine" or celebrate after each successful habit?

- Celebration creates positive emotion, teaches your brain to repeat the habit, and helps the habit stick faster.

## 4. Book Summary of Atomic Habits

### Question 5  
What is the book’s perspective about Identity?

- Identity-based habits focus on becoming the type of person who performs the habit, not just completing the habit itself.

### Question 6  
Book’s perspective on making a habit easier to do:

- Reduce friction  
- Prepare environment  
- Start small  
- Use two-minute rule  

### Question 7  
Book’s perspective on making a habit harder to do:

- Increase friction  
- Add steps or obstacles  
- Remove triggers from your environment  
- Make the process inconvenient

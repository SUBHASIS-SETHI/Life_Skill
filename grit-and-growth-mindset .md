# Grit and Growth Mindset

## 1. Grit

### Question 1  
Paraphrase the video in 1–2 lines.

- Grit means staying consistent and working hard for a long time to achieve goals.  
- Talent matters less than perseverance and long-term effort.

---

## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset

### Question 2  
Paraphrase the video in 1–2 lines.

- A growth mindset means believing that skills can be improved through effort and learning.  
- Failures become opportunities to grow instead of signs of weakness.

---

## 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control

### Question 3  
What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point?

- Believing that your actions, not outside forces, shape your success and future.  
- The key message: take responsibility and focus on what you can control.

---

## 4. How to Build a Growth Mindset

### Question 4  
Key points mentioned to build a growth mindset (no explanation).

- Embrace challenges  
- Learn from mistakes  
- Stay patient  
- Focus on progress  
- Replace negative self-talk  
- Celebrate small improvements  
- Keep practicing consistently

## 5. Mindset – MountBlue Warrior Reference Manual

### Question 5  
What are your ideas to take action and build a Growth Mindset?

- Accept mistakes and learn from them  
- Practice deliberately every day  
- Ask for feedback and use it  
- Stay curious and explore new concepts  
- Track progress to stay motivated  
- Replace “I can’t do this” with “I can learn this”

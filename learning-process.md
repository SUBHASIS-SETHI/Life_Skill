- Remove distractions

## Learning Principles in the Bootcamp

### Question 5  
What are some of the actions you can take going forward to improve your learning process?

- Study with full focus and no distractions  
- Switch between focused and relaxed thinking  
- Take notes in simple words  
- Break big concepts into small tasks  
- Practice regularly  
- Track time and reduce social media use  
- Teach concepts to confirm understanding
